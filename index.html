About Me

I'm a postgraduate student in the Jiangsu Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis Technology (B-DAT) at Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology (NUIST), supervised by Prof. Qingshan Liu. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and affective computing. Before coming to NUIST, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Shaanxi University of Technology.
